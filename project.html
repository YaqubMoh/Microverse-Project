About Dr. Norman Borlaug

Dr. Norman Borlaug, born in March 25, 1914, was an American agronomist, humanitarian and Nobel laureate who has been called "the father of the Green Revolution".

Borlaug is one of seven people to have won the Nobel Peace Prize, the Presidential Medal of Freedom and the Congressional Gold Medal and was also awarded the Padma Vibhushan, India's second highest civilian honor.
